What Happens If A Candidate Mistakenly Or Intentionally Rejects His Admission Offer On JAMB CAPS

It’s not the end if a candidate intentionally or mistakenly rejects his admission offer on Jamb CAPS. It’s a matter of choice. If a candidate is not satisfied with a course he is offered, he can reject it to be considered for admission in another institution. What happens if a candidate intentionally rejects his admission offer is the same as when he mistakenly does so.

However, when a candidate rejects his admission (intentionally or mistakenly) on Jamb CAPS, his profile will be moved to somewhere called “Market Place” on CAPS. This is designed to enabling other institutions that need students to pick from those who reject their admission offers, probably, due to their dissatisfactions towards the courses they are being offered in their first institutions of choice. When such a candidate happens to study what a particular institution needs and meets their requirements, he would be offered admission there. It’s rare.

NOTE: Your profile will be moved, only if you have rejected (intentionally or mistakenly) the admission you were offered by your first institution of choice.
