What Happens If A Candidate Fails To Accept/Reject Admission Offer On JAMB's CAPS

The responsibility of every UTME candidate is to either accept or reject his admission when offered on Jamb CAPS. But in a situation where such candidate fails to do so, due to his negligence or ignorance, these may happen:

1.The admission offer may be revoked: This is because a candidate offered admission is said to either accept or reject the offer within a certain period. Though Jamb may later reconsider him after they have absolutely done with the admission process of the particular year, it would be late and such candidate won’t meet up with his institution academic calendar.

In 2019, Unilorin and many institutions had nearly started conducting tests (academic tests) for the newly admitted candidates of the year before the names of candidates who were unable to accept their admission offers on CAPS were published by Jamb, and were asked to either accept or reject the offers. Those candidates had no choice than to reject/leave the offers, since they had already missed many lectures which might be hard for them to meet up. So, if you really need admission, stay informed.

2. The candidate won’t be considered for admission in other institutions: when a candidate happens to reject his admission offer, he will be made available to other institutions who are in need of students, otherwise he won’t be considered for admission. Such candidate will need to stay home for another 365days.
